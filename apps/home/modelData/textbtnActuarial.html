Actuarial Science

In simpler terms, Actuarial Science refers to a field of study where decision making is done based on probability, statistics and risk analysis.

Seems simple right? But actually it isn't that simple. Being an Actuary requires excellence in Statistics, Economics, Business & Mathematics. Because the nature of Actuarial problems is completely real and logical, that's the reason why it intrigues me to work for it.

I've cleared 3 Actuarial exams which taught me Statistics(Basic & Advanced), Probability(Basic & Advanced), Statistical Modelling, Insurance Modelling, Risk Analysis etc.
