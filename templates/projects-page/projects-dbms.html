Database Management Project

Title- Database Management of Online Multiplayer Gaming

Introduction

This idea of creating a database for online gaming seemed to be fresh and intereseting given that there were too many elements to access.

Methodology

  • Initially, sample data was collected and studied. The data consisted of players, weapons, locations
  • After that, relation between all these fields was studied and converted into relational format.
  • The relational structure was represented by ER diagram as well

Components

A typical multiplayer game consists of various features and the events that occur by the interaction of such features. Some of the concepts used:-
  • Triggers
  • Cursors
  • Procedures/Functions
