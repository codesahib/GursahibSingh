Android App Development Project

Title- Workout Assistant

Introduction

The app serves the purpose to easily track workouts by adding custom exercises. Workout volume is calculated by entering sets, reps and weight. This helps to track progresses in each workout session

Methodology

  • Android Studio used to build the app and also test on android emulator

Features

  • Simple and intuitive UI
  • Can add custom exercises (which may not be standard)
  • Easily divide exercises according to body part

Future Work

  • Suggestions for exercises based on body part
  • Track diet as well
  • Suggest workout plans based on personal goal
